About Me

ABOUT ME

Modulo21 (Andrea Strata, born 1996) is a multimedia artist, audio software developer
and researcher based in Italy. He is interested in researching new, experimental
ways to interact with sound, using sensors and other types of human-computer interfaces
to create experimental devices for sound installations and live performances.

SOFTWARE

  • KONKATENATOR

    A M4L device for corpus-based concatenative synthesis, based on the FluCoMa library.

    DEMO DOWNLOAD

  • SPECTRAL FREEZE

    A M4L Device that freezes the audio spectrum of any sound source, using jitter matrices to store FFT data. Based on the paper "A Tutorial on Spectral Sound Processing Using Max/MSP and Jitter" by Jean-Francois Charles.

    DEMO DOWNLOAD

  • AMBER

    Amplitude Modulation and Ring Modulation audio effect, developed in Max 4 Live.
